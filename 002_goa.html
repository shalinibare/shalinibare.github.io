A Quick Goa Getaway

A brief introduction to Goa:

Goa is a state on the southwestern coast of India. It is one of the biggest beach destinations in India, with a very vibrant and active nightlife. It is also very famous for its beachside shacks that provide a delicious variety of cuisine and beverage. I particularly favor the extreme land and water sports found in the region. For those who prefer a more laid back approach, there is a multitude of historical monuments and churches, and roadside markets that also are tourist hotspots.

Here are some of my favorite Goa gems!

  • Shiva Valley
  • Anjuna Beach
  • Tito's Shack
  • Brito's Shack
  • The German Bakery
  • Baga Beach
  • Cape Town
  • Vagator Beach
  • Curlies
  • Talassa
