A Quick Singapore Getaway

A brief introduction to Singapore:

Singapore is a Southeast Asian island city-state. I particularly like this location as it was the easiest to traverse, as not only is the entire island completely accessible through metro but also it only takes 45 minutes to go from one end of the island to the other. My absolute favorite is a certain region of the island that has different districts representing different cultures which makes it a wonderful way to explore the world on foot. Furthermore, Singapore is famous for its Botanical Gardens, Sentosa Island, Universal Studios, National University of Singapore, etc. Finally, the Singapore experience is not complete without eating from hawker stalls and trying Kaya Toast and chicken rice!

Here are some of my favorite Singapore gems!

  • Bugis Food Street + Market
  • Arab Street
  • Little India
  • Clarke Quay
  • The Old Police Station
  • Marquis
  • Haji Lane
  • Gardens By The Bay + Ferris Wheel
  • Tiong Bahru Bakery
  • Funan Mall
